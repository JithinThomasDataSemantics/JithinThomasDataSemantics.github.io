Saint Adjutor

A Norman lord, master of Vernonsur-Seine, who distinguished himself in the First Crusade. He was captured by the Muslims during the campaign but managed to escape from slavery. He returned to France and entered the abbey of Tiron. There he became a recluse, remaining recollected until his death.

He is the patron saint of swimmers, boaters and drowning victims. His feast day is celebrated on April 30.
