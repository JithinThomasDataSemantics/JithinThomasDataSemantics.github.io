Saint Drausinus

Drausinus was the bishop of Soissons, France. He established several foundations, even getting the tyrant Ebroin, who cared nothing for the Church, to help establish a chapel for sick nuns. Mediaeval legend says that to spend the night at Drausinus, tomb made one invincible; whole platoons of soldiers used to camp out at the tomb the night before a battle. His patronage also helps thwart the plans of your enemies. He is the patron saint of Champions,Invincible People and tobe invisible.

St Thomas Becket also visited the tomb before he returned to England' where he was murdered at the altar of Canterbury Cathedral, so you can't put all your faith in a saint's legend. and his feast day is celebrated on March 7th.
