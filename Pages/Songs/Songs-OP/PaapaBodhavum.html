Paapa Bodhavum


Paapa bodhavum paschaathaapavum
Karthaave enikkekane
Kanneerodum vilaapathodumen
Paapam njaanettu chollidaam

Neethi manyanaay annyare thaazhthi
Dhurvidhikal njaan cheyyilla
Paapa kaaranam annyaraanena
Nyaaya vaadhavum cheyyila

Aathma vanchana cheythu njaanente
Paapathe poozhthi vekkilla
Paapamethume ennilillennu
Chollum viddi njaanaakilla


Come, let us sing for joy to the Lord; let us shout aloud to the Rock of our salvation. Psalm 95:1
