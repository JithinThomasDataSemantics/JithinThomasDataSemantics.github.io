Oru Kodi Janmam


Oru kodi janmam ee
Bhoomiyil thannalum
Oru kodi naavennikkekiyalum(2)
Nira kodi nanmayam
Nin sthudhi paaduvan
Adiyeninnaavumo thamburane(2)

Oru kai kunjai, Pirannoru naalilum
Thiru karam thannilai, Kaathavan nee
Padharathe thalarathe,
Kai viral thumbinal,
Karam pidichenne nayichidhum nee(2)
Ente daivame, Ente snehame
Nanni njan chollunathngane(2)

Padhiganai papiyai, Paapathil veenitum
Palaviral thangi enne, thunachavan nee(2)
Paapamam lokathin, maayayil vezhadhe
Thirumaaril enne, ni kathidene (2)
Ente daivame Ente snehame
Nanni njan chollunathngane(2)


Come, let us sing for joy to the Lord; let us shout aloud to the Rock of our salvation. Psalm 95:1
