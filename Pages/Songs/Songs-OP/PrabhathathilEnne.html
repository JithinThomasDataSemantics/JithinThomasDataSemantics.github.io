Prabhathathil Enne


Prabhathathil enne samarppichu nadha
Vaazhthunnu njanum dhivyapadhanam
Itha innu ninte dhayadhikhyamallo
Unarthunnathenne anugrehichalum

Pithavinte munpil presadhicha puthra
Jeevante nadha ezhunnelliyalum
Sadha ente ullil prekashichu vaazhu
Vilangatte njanum nin jwalayayi

Prebho nandhiyode prekeerthidunne
Neeyanu nadhan njan ninte swantham
Sadha ninte sathyam prekhoshichu paadan
Irangatte vegam nee nayichalum


Come, let us sing for joy to the Lord; let us shout aloud to the Rock of our salvation. Psalm 95:1
