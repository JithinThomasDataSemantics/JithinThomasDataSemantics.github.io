Thrikkaikalil Poojya


Thrikkaikalil poojya paadhangalil
Kaanikkayaay swayam nalkunnu njaan
Aathmaarppanam ee hridhayaarppanam
Kaikkolluvaan kanivaakeename

Nin jeevanil divya virunnaakuvaan
Podiyunnu njaanum balivedhiyil
Thirumunpile mezhuthiriyaay njaan
Urukunnu naalam thelinjeeduvaan

Sahajaatharil ennum kaanunnithaa
Murivetta nin priya mukha shobha njaan
Aashwaasavum sneha peeyooshavum
Pakarnnekuvaan kripayekename


Come, let us sing for joy to the Lord; let us shout aloud to the Rock of our salvation. Psalm 95:1
