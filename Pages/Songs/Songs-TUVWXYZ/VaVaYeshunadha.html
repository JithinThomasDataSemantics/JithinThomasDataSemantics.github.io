Va Va Yeshunadha


Va va yeshu nadha
Va va sneha nadha
Haa enn hrithayam thedidum
Snehame nee
Vava yeshu nadha

Nee enn pranadhan
Nee enn sneharajan
Ninil ellam enn jeevanum snehame
Vava yeshu nadha

Paril illithupol Vannil illithu pol
Nee ozhinjullaraanatham chinthichedan
Vava yeshu nadha

Pookal killa prabha
Then mathuramalla
Nee varumbol enn anatham vanyamalla
Vava yeshu nadha

Venda pookarutha nadha nilkaneme
Theethul koolam nalloru poomandapam
Vava yeshu nadha.


Come, let us sing for joy to the Lord; let us shout aloud to the Rock of our salvation. Psalm 95:1
