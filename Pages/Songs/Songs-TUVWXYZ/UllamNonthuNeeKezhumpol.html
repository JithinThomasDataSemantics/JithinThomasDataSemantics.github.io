Ullam Nonthu Nee


Ullam nonthu nee thengumpol
Ullam kaiyil thagham njan
Ullile nombhara paadukalil
Chumpanameki thalodam njan (2)

Ente kunje ponnomale
Ninte daivam njan alleyo
Edaralle patharalle karayalle nee
Abhayamekan njan koodeyille

Aarokke ninne marannalum
Aarellam ninne veruthalum
Orikkalum ninne marakkilla njan
Oru vela polum piriyilla njan (2)

Roogiyayi nee theghi karayumpol
Paapiyayi neeyere thakarumpol
Aaswasamekuvan ananjidam njan
Athmeeya jeevan pakarnnidam njan (2)


Come, let us sing for joy to the Lord; let us shout aloud to the Rock of our salvation. Psalm 95:1
