Innen ullil vaniduvaan
Anayunna ente eshoye
Ninakkayi samarppikkum
Ente ee hrudayathe
Nirmmalamayi ennum kathidane

Paapi njan angil ninnum akalumpol
Patha katti nee nayikku satyapathayil
Ninte ishtam ennum ennil niravettuvaan
Ente ullil ennumennum vanidename

Snehashoonyanaayi njan alayumpol
Sneha dhaarayaayi nadhaa nee varename
Sneha deepamayi njan prakashichiduvaan
Mezhuthiripol uruki uruki theernniduvaan


Come, let us sing for joy to the Lord; let us shout aloud to the Rock of our salvation. Psalm 95:1
