Kaarunya Varidhe Nin





Karunya varidhe nin kaaltharilekidam
Kaazhchayayi enne njan snehapoorvam
Sweekarikaname naadha ente ekaazhchakal
Sampreethanakane ente ee baliyil (2)

Jeevanum enikkulla sarvavum nalkidam
Saadharam divyamee peelasayil (2)
Ente jeeva swasavum hrudaya veechi polum
Nalkidunnu poornamayi ee kaasayil








Come, let us sing for joy to the Lord; let us shout aloud to the Rock of our salvation. Psalm 95:1
