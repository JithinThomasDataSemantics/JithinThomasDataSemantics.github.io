Israyelin Nathanai


Israyelin nathanai vaazhum eka Daivam
Sathya jeeva maargamaanu Daivam
Marthyanayi bhoomiyil pirannu sneha Daivam
Nithya jeevan ekidunnu Daivam
Aba pithave Daivame
Aviduthe rajyam varename
Angayin thiruhitham bhoomiyil
Ennennum niraveridename

Chenkadalil nee annu paatha thelichu
Maruvil makkalkku manna pozhichu
Eriveyilil megha thanalayi
Irulil sneha naalamaay
Seenai maamala mukalil nee
Neethi pramanangal pakarnneki

Manujanai bhoovil avatharichu
Mahiyil jeevan bali kazhichu
Thiruninaum divya bhojyavumaay
Ee ulakathin jeevanaay
Vazhiyum sathyavum aayavane
Nin thirunaamam vaazhthunnu
