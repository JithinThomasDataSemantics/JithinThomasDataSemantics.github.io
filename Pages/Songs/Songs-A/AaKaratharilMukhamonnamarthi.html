Aa Karatharil


Aa karatharil mukhamonnamarthi
Onnu karayaan kazhinjirunnenkil
Thiruhrudaya karunya thanalil
Onnu mayangan kazhinjirunnenkil

Kaalvari naadha karunaamaya
Kaniyename sneha naadhaa

Ee jeevitha kurishinte bhaaram
Onnu thaangan kazhinjirunnenkil
Ee neerunna ormmakalellam
Onnu marakkan kazhinjirunnenkil

Aa krushitha rupathil nokki
Onnanuthapikkan kazhinjenkil
Aa vachanaghal anusarichennum
Onnu jeevikkan kazhinjirunnenkil


Come, let us sing for joy to the Lord; let us shout aloud to the Rock of our salvation. Psalm 95:1
