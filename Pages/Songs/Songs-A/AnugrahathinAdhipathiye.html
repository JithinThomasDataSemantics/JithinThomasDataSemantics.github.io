Anugrahathin


Anugrahathin adhipathiye
Ananda krupa perum nadiye
Anudinam Nin padam gethiye
Adiyanu Nin krupa mathiye

Van vinakal vanneedukil
Valayukayillen hridayam
Vallabhan Nee ennabhayam
Vanneedumo pinne bhayam

Thannuyire papikalkai
Thannavanam Nee iniyum
Thalleedumo ezha enne
Theerumo Nin snehamennil

Thiru karangal tharunna nalla
Sikshayil njan patharukilla
Makkalenkil sasanakal
Snehathin prekasanangal

Paridamam pazh manalil
Parthidum njan Nin thanalil
Marana dinam varumalavil
Maranjidum njan Nin marvidathil


Come, let us sing for joy to the Lord; let us shout aloud to the Rock of our salvation. Psalm 95:1
