Arhikkathathu Nalki


Arhikkathathu nalki ni enne
Andhanaakkarutheshuve
Arhikkunnathu nalkaathe naadhaa
Aarthanaakkaruthenne nee
Aashrayam ninte vankrupa
Aalambham ennum nin varam
Kaivalyam nalkum saanthwanam

Snehamen mathramen manasil
Satyam mathramen vachassil
Nanmakal mathram ninavin
Aathama chaithanyam vaazhvin
Neeyenikennum nalkane ennum
Nithimaanakum daivame

Paapathin irul vanathil
Paathakatti nee nayikku
Jeevithathinte nizhalil
Nithya shobhayaayi nirayu
Paaramel theertha kottayil ente
Maanasathil nee vazhane


Come, let us sing for joy to the Lord; let us shout aloud to the Rock of our salvation. Psalm 95:1
