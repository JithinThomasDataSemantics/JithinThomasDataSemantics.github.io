Arikil Varene


Arikil varene yeshunaadha
Abhayam tharene naayaka
En Maargame en jeevane
Ella navum pukazhthunna
Sneha saarame daivame

Paadipaadi vazhthuvan
Varangal needuvaan
Thedi vannivar sneha roopane
Rogangal akhilavum maaruvaan
Ennil varene karunayaal
Daivame nee varu ekidaam enneyum

Thedi thedi vannavan
Krupakal choriyuvaan
Chare ninnavan aake modhamaayi
Manasil nirayum daivame enne ariyum daivame
Naayakaa nee varu ekidaam poornnamayi


Come, let us sing for joy to the Lord; let us shout aloud to the Rock of our salvation. Psalm 95:1
