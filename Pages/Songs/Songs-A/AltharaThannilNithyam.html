Althara Thannil


Althara thannil nithyam vaazhum
Oo ente ponneshu naadhaa
Ennodonnayi onnu cheran
Ennullilennum vaazhan
Swargiya paathayil enne nayikkan
Nee varu raajadhi raja

Nee vannu cherunna neram en
Athmaavilanandha nimisham
Neeyen swargeeya bhagyam ennum
Nee mathramanente maargam

Naadha nin thiru hrudhayathil
Njanen jeevitham thrikaazhcha nalkaam
Aa thiru sannidhe ennum ente
Aaradhana pookkalekam



Come, let us sing for joy to the Lord; let us shout aloud to the Rock of our salvation. Psalm 95:1
