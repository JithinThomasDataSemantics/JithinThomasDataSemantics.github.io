Anaadhi Kaalam


Anaadhi kaalam munpe daivam
Ananthamaayi snehichu
Prapancham unarum munpe
Ninakku roopam kai vannu
Ninakku perumavan thannu

Jeevitha maargam athenthaayaalum
Kristhuvil evarum orupole
Anuthaapathaal mutti vilichaal
Rakshayavan tharum evarkkum

Kurishu chumakkunnavarude koode
Kristhuvumundaam orupole
Anuthaapathaal urukunnavarude
Hridhayam avanu graham pole


Come, let us sing for joy to the Lord; let us shout aloud to the Rock of our salvation. Psalm 95:1
