Anandha Snehame


Anandha snehame
Aaradhyamaam ange Naamam vaazhtham vaazhthidaam
Anudinam nin thiruhitham njan
Manasilennum kathidaam

Oro nimishavum orayiram sthuthi
Paadipukazhthunnu njangal
Ente daivame divya snehame
Ennumennum ninne mathram thedidunnitha

Thirunaama keerthanam paadan varunnitha
Nin divya gehathil njangal
Divya karunya naadhan nalkunna
Daanamennum maanasathil poomazhayaayi


Come, let us sing for joy to the Lord; let us shout aloud to the Rock of our salvation. Psalm 95:1
