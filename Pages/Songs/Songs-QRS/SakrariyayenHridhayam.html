Sakrariyayen


Sakrariyayen hrudhayam orukki kaathirippu (2)
Swargeeya raajakumara varane nee varane
En athmavin raajanayi vaazhan

Oo divyakarunyame o nithyasaayujyame
Oo snehavathsalyame en athmavil vaazhan varu (2)

Njananu jeevante appamennothi
Kurbaanayayi theernna naadha (2)
Jeevan tharan nithya jeevan tharan kudashayayi theernna naadha
Varane nee varane en athmavil raajanayi vaazhan

Njananu nalloridayanennothi
Maargam thelikkunna naadha (2)
Sneham tharan divya sneham tharan
Krushithanayi theernna naadha
Varane nee varane
En athmavil raajanayi vaazhan


Come, let us sing for joy to the Lord; let us shout aloud to the Rock of our salvation. Psalm 95:1
