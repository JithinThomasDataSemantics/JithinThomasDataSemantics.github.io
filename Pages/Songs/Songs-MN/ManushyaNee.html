Manushya Nee


Manushya nee mannaakunnu
Mannileku madangum nyoonam
Anuthaapa kannuneer veezhthi
Paapa parihaaram cheythu kolka nee

Bhalam nalkaathuyarnnu nilkkum
Vriksha nirayellaamarinju veezhthum
Eritheeyil erinju veezhum
Neeri niram maari chaambalaay theerum

Dhaiva puthran varunnoozhiyil
Dhaanya kalamellaam shuchiyaakkuvaan
Nenmanikal sambarikkunnu
Ketta pathirellaam chutterikkunnu

Aayirangal veenu thaazhunnu
Marthya maanasangal venthu neerunnu
Nithya jeevan nalkidum neerchaal
Vittu marubhoovil jalam thedunnu


Come, let us sing for joy to the Lord; let us shout aloud to the Rock of our salvation. Psalm 95:1
