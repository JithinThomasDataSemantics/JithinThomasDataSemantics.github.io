Nattucha Nerathu


Nattucha nerathu kinarinte theerathu
Vellathinayi njan kaathirippoo
Naari oru paathram dhaha jalam nee enikku nalkoo

Ayyayyo neeyoru dhoothan njan innoru samaryathi
Njan koriya vellam thottal theendalille

Ariyunnullethum nee ennodu nee chodhichal
Jeevante jalam kory tharumallo njan

Kayarilla paalayumilla neeyengane vellam korum
Jeevante jalam pinne engane kittum

Njan nalkum nithya jalam nee viswasamodinnu kudichal
Naree ninakkoru naalum dhahikkilla

Aa divya jalam nadha nalkenam enikkoru paathram
Veendum njan vellam koran porendallo

Makale nee veettil poy nin kanavaneyum kondu varoo
Appol njan kory vilambam jeevante jalam

Guruve nee kopikkaruthe veettil njan enthinu pokam
Illilla sathyam enikku bharthavilla

Nee chonnathu sathyam thanne kanavanmaranchundayi
Ippol ullavano ninte bharthavalla

Ivvidhathemn jeeva charithram neeyengane sarvam arinju
Daiva karam thelivay ninnil kaanunnu njan

Makale nee shankikkenda Daivathin puthran thanne
Nin munpil nilkkunnu nee arinju kolka

Nadha nin thiru mozhi kelkkan bhagyamenikkengane unday
Thruppadham viswasamode vanangidunne


Come, let us sing for joy to the Lord; let us shout aloud to the Rock of our salvation. Psalm 95:1
