Divyasnehame


Divyasnehame thirubhogyamayi nirayename
Divyakarunyame thirujeevanayi padarename
Nee varumpol en maanasathil
Snehapoorvam njaninnorukkam
Hrudayam niraye poomandapam
Theliyum prabha than pondeepakam

Snehame divyasnehame
nithyajeevanayi ennil vaazhane
Deepame satya deepame
divyashobhayal ennil theliyane

Naadha yeshunaadha ezhayam njan kaathu nilppu
Neerum ente dukham ninte munpil kazhchayayi ekam (2)
Ethu vare njan neeghi paapavazhiyil
Thiruvachanamathariyathere doore
Thedi vannu enne nee

Sneham divyasneham yeshunaadha nee mathramennum
Paapam neekkiyennil shanthithukum eka daivam (2)
Madhurageethameki vaazhthidum njan
Hrudayaveena meetti paadidum njan
Ennum sneha saanthanam
