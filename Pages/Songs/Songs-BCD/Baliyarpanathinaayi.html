Baliyarpanathinaayi


Baliyarpanathinaayi varunnu njangal
Sneharppanathinaay varunnu njangal
Paapam pokkum thirubaliyil anicheraan
Paapikal njangal ananjidunnu

Kurishil baliyay jeevan nalki
Raksha pakarnnu divya naadhan
Naadhaa savidhe anayunnivarum
Nin thiru baliyil pankucheraan

Bali theernaalum jeevitha vazhiyil
Bali chaithanyam pakarnnu neengaan
Sneham pakaraan thyagam cheyyaan
Ee baliyil varangalekoo


Come, let us sing for joy to the Lord; let us shout aloud to the Rock of our salvation. Psalm 95:1
