Divya Kaarunya


Divya kaarunya naayakane
Varu ennullam kaathirippu
Pon thoomanthahaasavumaay
Vinnin vaathil thurannu varoo

Kumbidunnu njangalithaa
Divya naayakaa savidhe

Ente aathmathin illaymayum
Ente akathaarin vallaymayum
Sarvameshuve marannennil vaazhaan
Ninnaagamam enthu bhaagyame

Kumbidunnu njangalithaa
Divya naayakaa savidhe

Ente vishwasa dhaarayinkal
Haa prathyaasha nee maathrame
Divya jyothise varikente chaare
En hrithadam venmayaakkidaan

Kumbidunnu njangalithaa
Divya naayakaa savidhe


Come, let us sing for joy to the Lord; let us shout aloud to the Rock of our salvation. Psalm 95:1
