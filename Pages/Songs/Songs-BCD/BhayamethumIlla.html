Bhayamethum Illa


Bhayamethum illente Daivam
Enne paripaalichu nadathum
Ananda thelineer cholayil
Anudinam vazhinadathum

Neeyallo nalla Idayan
Vazhi kaattum snehithan
Orshlem nayaka nin
Thirunamam paavanam

Dukhamillen priya Daivam
Ente vingunna nomparam neekkum
Kannir maaychente ullil
Ennum karunya poonthen niraykkum

Illa nirasha en Daivam enne
Thannullam kaikalil thangum
Swargathin vaathil thurakkum
Ennum sathyathiloode nayikkum


Come, let us sing for joy to the Lord; let us shout aloud to the Rock of our salvation. Psalm 95:1
