naakaloka naadhaneesho ezhunnallunnu
Maanavarkku varam thooki ezhunnallunnu

Bethlahemil vannudhichoru kanakathaaram
Yudhayaayil kathiru veeshiya parama dheepam
Unnathathil ninnirangiya divyabhojyam
Mannidathinu jeevanekiya swargabhojyam

Kaanayil vellam veenjaakkiyavan
Kadalinte meethe nadannu poyavan
Mrithiyadanja maanavarkku jeevaneki
Manamidinja rokikalkku saukyameki

Mahithale puthiya malarukalaninjeeduvin
Manujare mahitha geethikal pozhicheeduvin
Vairyavum pakayumellam maranneeduvin
Saadharam kaikal korthu niranneeduvin


Come, let us sing for joy to the Lord; let us shout aloud to the Rock of our salvation. Psalm 95:1
