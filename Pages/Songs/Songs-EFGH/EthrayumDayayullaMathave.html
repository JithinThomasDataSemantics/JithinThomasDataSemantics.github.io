Ethrayum Dayayulla


Ethrayum dayayulla mathave nin
Sanketham thedi varunnu njaghal
Nin charathodiyananjavare nee
Oru nalum kaividillallo thaye- (2)

Sharanam gamippu nin thrippadathil
Karunathan nirakudamakumamme- (2)
Kanivode evare nee kakkaname
Kanyakamarude raani neeye

Neduveerppum kannerum kaimuthalayi
Alayumi paapikal thanayarallo- (2)
Athirilla nin dayavaayppilitha
Abhayathinnanayunnu saadusheelar

Avatharam cheythoru vachanathinte
Amalayam ambhike annapoorne- (2)
Avaniyil sutharude yaachanakal
Alivode keetunin abhayameku


Come, let us sing for joy to the Lord; let us shout aloud to the Rock of our salvation. Psalm 95:1
