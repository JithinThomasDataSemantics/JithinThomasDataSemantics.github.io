Eesho Anayum


Eesho anayum paavana nimishamitha
Thiru jeevan pakarum nirmmala nimishamitha
Mama naadhanu sthuthi paadidaam
Mana veenakal meetti paadidam

Naadhaa varename ennil nirayename

En jeevitha dukhangal
Thiru kaikalilekidunnu
Ente rogavum ente vedhanayum
Thirupaadathilarppikkunnu

Naadha varename soukhyam pakaraname

En Bhaaviyum swapnangalum
Thiru kaikalilekidunnu
Ente beethiyum ente aashankayum
Thirupaadathilarppikkunnu

Naadha varename nervazhi kattename


Come, let us sing for joy to the Lord; let us shout aloud to the Rock of our salvation. Psalm 95:1
