En Yeshuve Nee


En yeshuve nee ethra nallavan
nee ethra kaarunyavaan
Nin snehamorthal entharalbhutham
ohh nithyamaam snehame(2)

Snehitharkku vendi swantha jeevane
Ekidunnathil param snehamo
Paapiyaaya marthyanaay polumaa
Jeevanekidum maha snehame

Vin mahathwam aake matti vechu nee
Mannithinte maalukal ettu nee
Marthyaroopamaarnnu dhaasanaayi nee
Mrithyu kaivarichoraa snehame


Come, let us sing for joy to the Lord; let us shout aloud to the Rock of our salvation. Psalm 95:1
