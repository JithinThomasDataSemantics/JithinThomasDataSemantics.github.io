En Yeshu Naadhante


En yeshu naadhante paadhathinkal njaan
Ini ennalumee mannil jeevichidum
Enthoram kleshangal nerittaalum njan
Ente karthaavin snehathilaananthikkum
Dhoore pokunna nimishangalil
Thedi paanjethumidayanavan
Aarum kaanathe thalarnnidumpol
Tholilenthi thaan thazhukidunnu
Swarga seeyonil naadhane kaanmathinaay
Ente aathmaavu daahichu kaathirippoo

Aareyum njaan bhayappedilla
Ente karthaaven koode vannaal
Illa thaazhukil njaan thakarukilla
Ennum thannodu chernnu ninnal
Yaathrayil njaan Thalarnnidumpol
Ennathma dhairyam chornnidumpol
Raathrikaale nadungidumpol
En meniyaake virachidumpol
Shobhithamam thirumukhamen
Ullil kannale kaanunnathen bhaagyam
Paadidum njaan sthuthi vachanam
Thante simhaasanathinkal raajanu njaan

Bhoovilaanen bhavanamennu
Alpa viswasi njaan karuthi
Swarga veettilellam orukki vechu
Ente nalleshu kaathirippoo
Krooshilevam sahichuvallo
En kleshabhaaram akattiduvaan
Praanangu samarppichallo
Ennaathma rakshaa vazhi thelikkaan
Thedukilla njaan jadika sugham
Ini njaanalla jeevippatheshuvathre
Paadidum njaan sthuthivachanam
Thante simhaasanathinkal rajanu njaan
