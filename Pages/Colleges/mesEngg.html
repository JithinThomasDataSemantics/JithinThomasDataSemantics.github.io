MES College of Engineering, commonly known as MES Kuttipuram, established as an institution with minority status in 1994, is the first self-financing engineering college in the state of Kerala. The college, located along the banks of the Bharathapuzha River near Kuttippuram town. It is 72 Km South of Calicut.
